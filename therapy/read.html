Reading therapy

Health practitioners use books and bibliotherapy to support the mental health of
groups facing various challenges, including anxiety, depression, and grief.

Benefits of Reading:

  1. Reduces stress.
  2. Increases your ability to empathize.
  3. Helps prevent age-related cognitive decline.
  4. Builds your vocabulary.
  5. Prepares you for a good night’s rest.
  6. Helps alleviate depression symptoms.
